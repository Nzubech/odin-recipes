Egusi Recipes

Description

Egusi soup is native food in West-Africa (Nigeria). It is eaten with Garri, Fufu or Semovita, and its also nutritious to the body. Egusi is fortunately my best soup and it goes with goat meat to add more taste to the soup. Try preparing it and enjoy the taste.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pumpkin seeds

  • Salt to taste

  • 1/2 cup peanut oil
